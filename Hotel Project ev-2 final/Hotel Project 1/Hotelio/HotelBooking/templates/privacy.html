Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 14, 2024

Last Updated: February 17, 2025

Welcome to HOTELIO. Your privacy is our priority. This policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you use our platform and services.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Name, email address, and phone number
  • Payment details (credit/debit card information)
  • Booking history and preferences
  • Identification documents (if required for booking)

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device details (IP address, browser type, operating system)
  • Usage data (pages visited, time spent on the platform)
  • Location data (if enabled by your device settings)
  • Cookies and tracking technologies (see Cookies Policy)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To process and confirm hotel bookings
  • To provide customer support and improve your experience
  • To send booking confirmations and policy updates
  • To detect and prevent fraud and security threats
  • To comply with legal and regulatory requirements

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal data. However, we may share information with:

  • Hotels & Service Providers: To fulfill your bookings
  • Payment Processors: To process transactions securely
  • Legal Authorities: When required by law
  • Marketing Partners: Only with your consent

4. Data Retention & Security

  • We retain your data as long as necessary for business and legal purposes.
  • Advanced encryption and security protocols are used to safeguard your information.
  • While we take necessary precautions, no online platform is 100% secure.

5. Your Rights & Choices

  • Access & Correction: Request access to or correction of your data.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data (subject to legal requirements).
  • Opt-Out: Unsubscribe from marketing emails anytime.
  • Cookies Control: Manage cookies through browser settings.

To exercise these rights, contact us at hotelio.inn@gmail.com.

6. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies to:

  • Enhance website performance
  • Personalize content
  • Analyze user behavior

You can manage cookie preferences in your browser settings.

7. Third-Party Links

Our platform may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices. Please review their policies before sharing any data.

8. International Data Transfers

If you access our services from outside India, your data may be transferred and processed in jurisdictions with different data protection laws.

9. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Significant changes will be communicated via email or platform notifications.
